Network of Education
"Link the best education services to everyone."

What NOE can do?

Find the people who want to go to the restaurant but don't want to go alone.

Schedule the dinner and eat together in your favorite restaurant with the person you like.

Make friends with people through the dinner and share good eating places.

How to use NOE?

  • 1

    User Registration

    Open this web page or install NOE app on your mobile device. Sign up as a new RestP user if you first use it for the first time. Otherwise login in with your existing account.

  • 2

    Find & Respond to Invitations

    The app shows a list of invitations for dinner. Each one contains details of its inviter and target restaurant. Add filters and pick your interested invitation. You will be accepted if the inviter like you too.

  • 3

    Create Your Invitation

    You can also create your invitation that will be visible to others. When your invitation is responded by someone, you'll get notified to decide whether to accept or decline this person by checking his/her profile and rating.

  • 4

    Have Dinner Together

    After the invitation is done, you can go to the target restaurant, meet, talk and share your favorite food with your dinner partner(s).

  • 5

    Rate Each Other

    Have dinner and make friends with your partner. After the dinner, you can rate each other. Higher rating gives you better chance to be selected in the next run, so be nice and make every dinner a pleasant time! :-)
